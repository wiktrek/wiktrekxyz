# How do I study?
## Think 
1. Think why do you have to learn this thing you want to learn.
2. Think about what you want to learn. For example if I wanted to learn multiplication I would have to learn addition first.
3. Pick the best studying method for the topic you want to learn or the method you like the most
## Check
Check your knowledge. 
Compare results before and after if you're not improving, try to figure out why or go ask someone
## Studying methods
My favorite methods are:
- Teaching someone - I think Its the best studying method because not only you gain something from it but someone else does too. 
When you teach someone you force yourself to think carefully so you don't explain something wrong.
- Do practice problems.
- Active recall - Force yourself to remember things for example: read notes, take a quiz, write down everything you remembered.
- Spaced repetition - You study the same material in multiple sittings not just one. For example: day 1 -> day 3 -> day 7 -> day 30.
- Interleaving - study multiple topics for example: 30 min math, 30 min informatics.
- Visualizing - visualize the thing you want to learn.
- Feynman method - explain the topic as if you were explaining it to a 5 year old.
## Other important things
1. Sleep - helps with almost everything
2. Remove distractions
